Notes, Tips and Misc Info

  • We do not have a minimum order, but orders under $7 will be charged a $1.00 fee (does not apply to orders being shipped).
  • Our products are divided into Product Catalogs - one for each of our main paper types and services. You cannot order from more than one catalog in the same order.
  • Our pricing for Pro Luster/Glossy/Pearl prints is quantity based with breaks for over 10, 100 and over 300 prints of the same size. It does not matter if you order the same image or different images, as long as they are the same size print you will get the quantity price for the total number of prints.
  • Our normal turnaround time is 24 hours on standard prints and press products, 2 days for metal or tile and 3-4 days for canvas wraps.
  • We ship via USPS Priority and Express Mail for most orders - they have been excellent to work with. If you would like to use your own UPS/Fed Ex account please let us know. For large mounted prints and canvas wraps we use UPS ground.
  • Having trouble ordering large quantities of different images? ROES has trouble with our way of pricing (quantity breaks for multiple images for the same size). If ROES locks up or slows to a crawl, try the Quantity Proofs tab (inside the Pro Luster Catalog). This catalog is set to the 300 + price and works well with large quantites. If you use it and have less than 300 of any one size, the price displayed in ROES will be wrong. You will be charged the correct price regardless of what ROES displays.
  • ROES will only accept jpg images. If you need to send tif or other file types, please use our FTP2Lab ftp service.
  • File names should not contain anything other than letters, numbers, dashes or underscores. Special characters like #, ' ? or $ will cause problems when we process your order. Images that are B&W should still be rgb and not grayscale.
