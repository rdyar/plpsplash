We have 2 paper types available - both are traditional silver halide papers (not inkjet) made by Fuji.

  • Crystal Archive (8x10 only, glossy or luster) This is Fuji's consumer paper called Fuji Crystal Archive. This is available only for our $.70 8x10 prints. Crystal Archive is great paper, but the Pro Luster is better.
  • Pro Luster (4x6 up to 12x18): This is Fuji's Professional paper called Fuji Super Type PD. It is a great all around paper with a thick base, deep blacks and a professional watermark on the back. Luster is a mostly matte finish with a very slight texture to it.
